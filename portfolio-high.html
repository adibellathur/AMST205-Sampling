High - Young Thug


  Another great example of an song using Sampling to conenct different generations together is High by Young Thug, featuring Elton John. The producer of the record, Stelios, actually had the idea to connect these 2 artists together, saying "[Young Thug's] style has always been super flamboyant and expressive and exciting and Elton has had the same thing. I thought it would be a super positive thing to just see them together and see 2 icons embrace each other." Both Young Thug and Elton John are well-known trailblazers in their respective genres, not afraid to go against the grain. Elton John is known for his classically inspired meolodies, his unique songwriting choices, and his flamboyant fashion choices. Young Thug has become well known in more recent times for his unique melodies shaped by his yelping, singsong voice, and also his unique fashion choices (his album Jeffrey has him wearing a dress in the cover art). So connecting the 2 for this song was a clear message that these 2 are from the same lineage of musical trailblazers. Their story is connected, despite being years and genres apart.
