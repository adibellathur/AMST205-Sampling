Midsummer Madness - 88Rising


  Another Instance of Sampling connecting an artist to their roots is 88Rising, and their biggest hit, Midsummer Madness. 88Rising is a "hybrid management, record label, video production, and marketing company", founded by Sean Miyashiro. They feature a collection of mainly Asian American artists, the most well-known of which include NIKI, Keith Ape, Rich Brian, and Joji. In this particular song, they sample the song Home, by Jang Hyun Seung. It is fitting that the biggest song for a collective who’s aim it is to empower Asians and Asian American artists samples a relatively unknown Asian artist. 88Rising was able to extend the vision of their group to even help artists who aren’t directly signed on to their label.

  An interesting wrinkle in this story is that many claim that this sound was found in a sample pack on splice.com. Splice is a website where artists can get sounds for a low price, and fairly compensate artists for being sampled. It says a lot about the state of modern music production that there are entire websites dedicated to spreading sounds and samples among members of the music production community. Sampling has come a long way since it was just a way to extend disco beats. It is an integral part of music creation and an artform within itself.
