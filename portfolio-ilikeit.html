I Like It - Cardi B


  Artists are not only using more samples, but using samples that have a personal connection. Cardi B, rapper from Bronx, sampled 60's latin boogaloo classic I Like It Like That by Pete Rodriguez, to make her hit song I Like It. The song was a powerful way to bring her Latin-American heritage to mainstream american pop music. In fact, when asked about the new song, he mentioned that he only realized he was on the radio again when his grandchildren called him. He later said, "This is crazy. I never thought it was going to last for 50 years." Through the power of sampling, music from 60 years ago has found new life among a new generation, keeping the tradition of music alive.
